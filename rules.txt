Rules:
-Monster: ♣, ♠, Potion: ♥, Weapon: ♦.
-Resolve room by clicking 3 cards.
-You may redraw the room by right
clicking the pile, two redraws in
a row are not allowed.
-Weapons can kill only monsters of
equal or lower strength to the last
killed monster by the weapon.
-Right click a monster with a weapon
equiped to use hands instead.
-Resolve all cards in the deck to win.
If less than 3 cards remain in the deck
after clearing the room you win on the spot.
